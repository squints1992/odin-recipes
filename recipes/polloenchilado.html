Pollo Enchilado

This is recipe is simple, but what makes it one of my favorites is the marinade.
I'm not competely sure if my mom came came up with this marinade, but i'd like think so.
She is the best mom ever. If youre in the gym and trying to get your protein intake,
but are so tired of the bland chicken. This one is for you. I hope you enjoy!

Ingredients
  • 4lbs chicken leg & thigh
  • 8 Chiles Guajillo
  • 1/2 tsp of Comino
  • 6 Garlic teeth
  • 1/2 tps pepper
  • 6 cloves
  • Salt

Steps
  1. Toast the 8 Chile Guajillo.To toast them, heat a pan to medium-hot.
    Place the chiles the pan and toast them for about 20 to 30 seconds per side until they start to become fragrant.
  2. Once tosted toss them in a blander with the rest of the ingredients. Except the chicken, obviously.
  3. Add 1/2 a cup of vinegar. 1 1/2 cup of water.
  4. Blend for aboaut 45 sec.
  5. Throw the chicken into a pot. Add the blended contents into pot with chicken.
  6. Make sure the entire chicken is covered and let sit for 2-3hrs.
  7. Grill chicken thoroughly, enjoy!
