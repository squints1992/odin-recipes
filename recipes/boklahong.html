Bok LaHong

This recipe is not exactly a main dish it is a Cambodian salad, but it can definitely be eaten by its self.
This salad is packed with lots of flavors. I recommend eating with Lemon Grass beef sticks with white rice.

Ingredients
  • 1 lb of Shredded green papaya (450 gram)
  • 5 Salty baby crabs from the Asian store break apart
  • 2 tablespoon of lime juice
  • 2 Thai chilies
  • 1 to 2 tablespoon of Tuk prahok or cooked prahok
  • 2 tablespoon of dried shrimps
  • 1 liced of tomato or cherries tomatoes
  • 2 long beans cut to 1 inch long
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce more or less up to you
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tablespoon of palm sugar ( this is optional) put it last if you need it
  • 1 to 2 tablespoon of crust roasted peanut for garnish

Steps
  1. In a deep mortar Pestle pound the garlic, chilies, to paste.
  2. Now add shredded green papaya, lime juice dried shrimps, and long bean, pound it until it is soft
  3. Then add the rest of the ingredients taste for salty, sour and spicy according to your taste. Garnish with crush peanut.
