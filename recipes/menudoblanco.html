Menudo Blanco

Menudo can be considered a delicacy. It is the stomach lining of a cow many people can be
put off by it because of its texture, it can be very chewy. If cooked correctly it melts in
in your mouth and very flavorfull. If you can get passed the texture you will not
regret it. After a heavy night of drinking it is a great hang over food. I hope you enjoy!

Ingredients
  • 4 lbs of tripe
  • 1 onion
  • 6 Garlic teeth
  • Yerba Buena (Mint Leaf)
  • Salt (to your liking)

      • Condiments
    • Chopped Purple Onions
    • Oregano
    • Chile Piquin
    • Chile Serrano
    • Lime

Steps
  1. Drain and rinse beef honeycomb tripe; place in a large pot or bowl.
    Squeeze lime juice over tripe, and fill pot with water.
    Let tripe soak in lime juice and water solution for 5 minutes; drain and rinse again.
    This step will help eliminate any unpleasant odor from tripe
  2. Place tripe in pot. cut the onion in half throw it in the pot along with garlic teeth
    and 10 Yerba Buena leafs, also salt to your liking.
  3. Fill up pot with water until tripe is completly submereged.
  4. Boil for 4-5hrs on medium heat.
  5. Serve in Bowls. enjoy!
